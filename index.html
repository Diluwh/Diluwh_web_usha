About USHA Karate Team

As USHA team, we have gathered children from Anuradhapura, Thabuttegama and Rajanganaya villages to become a stronger and more powerful group. We train under K.M. Shelton Anil Kumara. There are currently only 300 school children in our group. There are also a number of senior students who have achieved black belt. Our group also has national and international achievements, you can see them on this site.

Here are ten reasons why students should learn karate:

  1. Mental Strength - Karate helps in developing mental strength and discipline, which can enhance focus and determination in academic and personal life.
  2. Social Skills - Practicing karate improves social interactions and teamwork, helping students build better relationships and communication skills.
  3. Physical Fitness - Regular training in karate enhances physical fitness, strength, and overall health.
  4. Attention and Concentration - Karate training requires concentration and attention to detail, which can improve students' ability to focus in their studies.
  5. Presentation Skills - Students gain confidence and poise, which can be beneficial for presentations and public speaking.
  6. Bone Health - Engaging in karate strengthens bones and improves bone density, contributing to better bone health.
  7. Career Guidance - Karate can provide opportunities for career guidance and professional development within the martial arts field.
  8. Self-Discipline and Respect - Karate teaches self-discipline and respect for others, which can positively influence behavior and attitudes.
  9. Stress Relief - The physical activity and mental focus involved in karate provide effective stress relief and relaxation.
  10. Health Awareness - Practicing karate promotes awareness of physical health and well-being, encouraging students to adopt a healthier lifestyle.
